Welcome to Cafe n' chill

Ideal for a breakfast bite, your morning coffee, a sumptuous lunch or afternoon snack attack, cafe n' chill ensures efficient table service alongside a grab-and-go selection of house-made confections, sandwiches and light bites. Our specialty coffees are custom roasted locally by Caffè Umbria and served by a team of highly trained baristas.

Find a hand-chosen selection of flavorsome, fresh, savory and sweet offerings from the kitchen of Chef David Hawksworth. Whether it’s a quick bite over custom coffee blends on the way to work or a refreshing light lunch after a visit to the shops or nearby Vancouver Art Gallery, Bel Café is sure to delight and inspire.
